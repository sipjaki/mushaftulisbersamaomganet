About

A Business Analyst bridges the gap between business needs and technology solutions. They analyze data, identify business challenges, and propose strategic solutions to improve efficiency, profitability, and decision-making.

Responsibilities

Client Assessment & Strategy Development

  • Gather and analyze business requirements from stakeholders.
  • Identify areas for process improvement and efficiency.
  • Develop reports, dashboards, and visualizations using tools like Excel, Power BI, or Tableau.
  • Collaborate with IT and development teams to implement solutions.
  • Perform market and competitive analysis.
  • Document business processes and recommend improvements.
  • Conduct risk analysis and assess project feasibility.

Skills

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Experience with data analysis tools (Excel, SQL, Power BI, Tableau).
  • Knowledge of business process modeling (BPMN, UML).
  • Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills.
  • Understanding of Agile and Scrum methodologies.

Educational & Experience Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business, Finance, IT, or a related field.
  • 1-3 years of experience in business analysis, consulting, or a related role.
  • Certifications like CBAP (Certified Business Analysis Professional) or PMI-PBA are a plus.

Salary Range:

  • $60,000 – $90,000 per year (varies by location and industry).
